Sakoon Care - Caregiving Agency Website

This project involved developing a comprehensive website for Sakoon Care, a caregiving agency dedicated to providing compassionate and professional care services. The website features a user-friendly interface for clients to explore services, read testimonials, and easily get in touch. Key functionalities include service descriptions, an online inquiry form, and a blog section for caregiving resources. The design emphasizes warmth, trustworthiness, and ease of navigation to support families in finding the right care solutions.
